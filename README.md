Project Title/Headline --
Sales and Profit Data Analysis Dashboard

Introduction/Purpose --
A Sales and Profit Dashboard summarizes key business performance by visualizing metrics like total revenue, profit margins, top products, and sales team performance, for quick decisions, using charts, KPIs, and filters to show trends by region, category, or time period, helping identify growth areas and inefficiencies. High-Level KPIs (Top Cards): Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Average Order Value, Number of Orders.Revenue Breakdown by Product Category/Brand by Region/Country. Monitor Performance for get real-time insights into sales goals. Identify Trends Spot patterns in customer behavior or product demand.Improve Forecasting on make data-driven predictions. Pinpoint high-margin products and profitable customer groups. Enhance Strategy: Adjust sales tactics and resource allocation.
